About    

Little Forest is a 2018 South Korean drama film directed by Yim Soon-rye and based on a manga series of the same name by Daisuke Igarashi which was first published in 2002 . After failing to pass the national qualification exam to become a teacher, Hye-won (Kim Tae-ri) abandons her part-time job in Seoul to return to the small village where she was raised.He story of a young woman who returns to her childhood home, in a traditional Korean village, after leaving for the big city in pursuit of what turned out to be an elusive dream.


 

Review

Kim Tae-ri shines as Song Hye-won, who returns from Seoul to her rural family home after pursuing a teaching certificate. Her father has passed, and her mother, played by Moon So-ri, left before Hye-won went to college. Raised with a passion for cooking and agriculture, Hye-won reconnects with childhood friends Joo Eun-sook (Jin Ki-joo), who works at a bank and longs for city life, and Lee Jae-ha (Ryu Jun-yeol), who left Seoul to become a farmer. Beautifully shot scenes of nature, heartfelt meals, and the nuanced mother-daughter conflict enhance this well-acted drama, with Kim Tae-ri standing out as a leading actress.
