GOBLIN:The Lonely and Great God   2016

GENRE                         Fantastic,Comedy,Romance
DIRECRED BY            Lee Eung Bok
WRITTEN BY              Kim Eun Sook
EPISODES                    16
STARRING                   Gong Yoo, Kim Go Eun, Lee Dong Wook, Yoo In Na, Yook Sung Jae



About    

Guardian: The Lonely and Great God," also known as "Goblin," is a mesmerizing South Korean drama that has taken the world by storm. Kim Shin (Gong Yoo), a decorated military general from the Goryeo Dynasty, is framed as a traitor and killed by the young king. Years after his death, he is cursed by the Almighty to stay immortal forever, enduring the pain of seeing his loved ones die as punishment for the soldiers he killed to protect his country. He becomes an immortal goblin, helping people with his powers and being a kind man in spite of his grieving past. The only way to put an end to his immortality is the Goblin's bride, whose aid in pulling out the sword will end his painful immortality.Ji Eun-tak (Kim Go-eun) is a bubbly high school student who remains cheerful and hopeful despite her tragic life and can see ghosts, though she tries to ignore them. She summons the Goblin by chance and their fates begin to entwine. The Goblin's nephew, Yoo Deok-hwa (Yook Sung-jae), leases the Goblin's house to a Grim Reaper (Lee Dong-wook) and the two end up living under the same roof. Ji Eun-tak starts working a part-time job at a chicken store run by a charismatic young woman, Sunny (Yoo In-na).As the lives of Kim Shin, Grim Reaper, Ji Eun-tak and Sunny interweave, a deeper story unfolds, as they are not just strangers who met by chance, but people with deep-rooted relations.


  

Review

This enchanting masterpiece effortlessly weaves together fantasy, romance, and folklore in a way that will leave you spellbound.The storytelling in "Goblin" is nothing short of exceptional. The plot revolves around a cursed goblin (Gong Yoo) and his quest for redemption, as well as his heartwarming relationship with the quirky Grim Reaper (Lee Dong Wook) and the endearing human Ji Eun Tak (Kim Go Eun). Each episode is a rollercoaster of emotions, seamlessly transitioning from heart-wrenching to hilarious moments, all while maintaining a sense of magical wonder.The breathtaking cinematography and stunning visuals transport viewers to a world where the line between reality and fantasy blurs. The picturesque landscapes and the captivating portrayal of traditional Korean culture add depth to the narrative.The performances of the cast are nothing short of remarkable, with Gong Yoo's charismatic presence, Lee Dong Wook's impeccable comic timing, and Kim Go Eun's genuine innocence stealing the show. The chemistry between the leads is palpable, making their love story truly unforgettable.blin is a must-watch for anyone in search of a gripping, heartwarming, and visually stunning drama. It's a story that will stay with you long after the final episode, leaving you with a sense of awe and a belief in the enduring power of love and friendship. Don't miss this enchanting journey; it's a true gem in the world of television
